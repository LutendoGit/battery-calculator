## Video Content Ideas

**Title:** Power vs Energy — The Most Important Concept in Battery Sizing

**Duration:** 3:15 minutes

**Script Outline:**
1. Introduction (0:00-0:30)
   - Hook: "If you get this wrong, your battery system will fail"
   - Preview: Power vs Energy explained with real examples

2. Power Explained (0:30-1:30)
   - Definition: Rate of electricity use
   - Analogy: Width of water pipe
   - Examples: Kettle (2kW), microwave (1.2kW), geyser (3-4kW)
   - Visual: Show appliances with power ratings

3. Energy Explained (1:30-2:30)
   - Definition: Total electricity stored/used over time
   - Analogy: Size of water tank
   - Examples: 10kWh battery can supply 1kW for 10 hours, 2kW for 5 hours
   - Visual: Battery capacity demonstrations

4. Real Installer Example (2:30-3:00)
   - House with 1kW essential loads
   - 5-hour loadshedding scenario
   - Calculation: 1kW × 5hrs = 5kWh needed
   - Important: Never size for 100% discharge

5. Conclusion (3:00-3:15)
   - Key takeaway: Power determines inverter size, energy determines battery size
   - Call to action: Practice calculations
