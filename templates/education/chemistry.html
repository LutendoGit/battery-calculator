💡 Choosing the Right Chemistry

  • Energy Storage Systems: LiFePO4 (safe, long life, can do 100% DOD)
  • Consumer Electronics: Li-ion/NCM (high energy density)
  • Electric Vehicles: NCA or NCM (power and energy balance)
  • High-Power Applications: NCA (highest energy density)
  • Critical Applications (Medical): LiFePO4 (safest option)
