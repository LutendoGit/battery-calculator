Welcome to the Revov WattWorks Foundation Installer Training Program

Welcome to WattWorks Foundation Installer Training, the starting point for becoming a REVOV Certified Installer.

This is where real installation knowledge begins.

The WattWorks Foundation Installer Program is designed to give you the technical understanding, practical confidence and system-level thinking required to install battery-based energy systems safely, correctly and professionally.

Whether you are new to battery installations or already working in the field and want to sharpen your fundamentals, this program will strengthen your core knowledge, so you can install it right the first time.

What You’ll Gain from WattWorks Foundation Installer Training Program
By completing this training program, you will understand:
  • How complete energy systems actually work
  • The role of each component within a battery-based installation
  • Safe installation practices and compliance basics
  • Core system sizing principles
  • How Revov Lithium-Ion batteries integrate into systems
  • Proper commissioning procedures
  • How to identify and prevent common installation errors
This is not just theory.
WattWorks is practical, field-relevant training built around real installation scenarios, helping you reduce call-backs, avoid costly mistakes and build installer confidence.
How WattWorks Foundation Installer Program Works
The Foundation program is structured into focused modules, each covering a key part of the installation process.
You can:
🔹 Complete the modules in sequence (recommended to build strong foundational knowledge)
OR
🔹 Navigate between modules based on your learning needs
At the end of each module, you will complete a short assessment quiz to confirm your understanding.
Once all modules and quizzes are successfully completed, you will earn your: WattWorks Foundation Installer Certificate.
